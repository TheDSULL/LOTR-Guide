They make it to Mt. Doom just in time, where Gollum tries to stop them, but now Sam has mercy. Frodo dosen't give up the Ring, but Gollum takes it from him and falls into the volcano, destorying the Ring. Sauron is defeated and Gandalf rescue's Sam and Frodo from the slopes of Mt. Doom. Sam and Frodo are healed by Aragorn, who is crowned new King of Gondor, marrying Elronds daughter Arwen. Eventually Frodo and the hobbits depart north, passing through everything, and they meet Saruman on the road. Then they meet Bilbo again and he askes for Frodo to finish his writings. Passing through Bree they discover the troubles that have harmed the North, and learn not all is well in the Shire. They find the Shire under the rule of crule men, and they themselves raise the Shire and fight these overlords, before finding out it was Saruman who was there master, then he is killed. The Hobbits set out and heal all the hurt done in the Shire, and Sam marries Rosie. But Frodo was still troubled by the hurts he had recived along the road, and at last departs middle Earth with Bilbo, Gandalf, and many important Elves.
