Writing Style Guide

By Morgor-MFAVF

What I love most about this book is the writing, which I can break down into three reasons

  1. The Language
  2. The Depth
  3. The Descriptions

Langague

The word choice in this book is great and you really feel the influences of old english on it. Through Tolkeins own obbsession with Language's he made many for the story, which all add even more to it.

Depth

In his writing you can sense the great depth of thought he put into the history of the world and all it's people and cultures. This makes reading it also feel mysterious at times as you seem to be walking through an even more ancient past. This makes it so there is always something new to find in every read.

Descriptions

By romero-leo

A lot of this book is descriptions of the lands sourrounding those journeying, and whilst it shows the depth of the descriptions, it is also great in its own right. The joke is that Tolkien spends an entire page describing a tree, but it is done so beautifully that it becomes some of the best parts of the story, even with all else.
